FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. How much is the full course?

Our course consultant will explain to you in details regarding the course after you have left your contact information or when you have called our centre.

2. How long does it take to complete the course?

A 90 lesson course takes about 5 months – 18 months to complete depending on how you schedule the classes for your child

3.If my child is younger than 3 years old or older than 14 years old, are they still eligible to join the programme?

Unfortunately, our course is tailored for children whom are the age of 3 years old, to 14 years old. If your child is older than 14 years old, then they will no longer be eligible for our programme.

4. How many times a week/month does my child need to attend the class?

Our schedule is flexible, you can have a full weeks’ worth of classes or attend one class per week. It is up to you to decide how many classes per week you want for your child, though we recommend 3 classes per week to better retain the things that they have learn in class

5. Do you provide a transportation service?

Currently, we do not provide any transportation service. If you think our branches are too far from your house, do drop us a feedback to let us know where you want us to open our next branch!
