Cambridge English Assessment

Our Cambridge YLE Course is a three-level English programme that engage children in 21st century learning. Designed to be fun and engaging, the course teaches children English by getting them to speak and write about their real-life experiences.

A fun and engaging learning process enjoy will motivate kids to commit to any task and achieve more success at the end of the day. Learners are advised to first take a placement test to determine which course is appropriate to their level of English language proficiency.

Additionally, a Cambridge English certificate will grant your child access to the best study, life and work opportunities. Learners are advised to first take a one-hour placement test at any KLC Centre to determine their level of English language proficiency.

Cambridge English Assessment Categories:

Pre A1 Starters

5 - 8 years old

A1 Movers

9 - 10 years old

A2 Flyers

11 - 12 years old

A2 Key

13 - 14 years old

B1 Preliminary

15 - 16 years old
