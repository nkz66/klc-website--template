Phonic Programme (4 - 14 years old)

Phonic Programme is a reading foundation for children, with a method that’s tried and proven effective. Children learn how letters combine to create invariant sounds, which empowers them to take charge of their own reading progress.

Through this programme, children can read intuitively without memorizing vocabulary, as well as advance their reading ability and critical thinking. They’ll be able to master reading concepts on their own , thus increasing their confidence and engagement with books. Upon completion of the Phonic programme, children will be able to “read by eye” and “spell by ear”.

Programme features :
  • Able to do spelling with minimal practice
  • Able to read story books instead of just looking at pictures
  • Develop confidence to speak English
