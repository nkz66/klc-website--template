APA KATA IBU BAPA SELEPAS MENDAFTARKAN ANAK DI KLC

Chen Nan Yu

Before Yan Bin joined KLC, he had mastered the pronunciation of the alphabets. However, he couldn’t pronounce longer words and he was not confident in reading. Therefore, I decided to sign up a course for him in KLC in April, 2017. Yan Bin had already went for 11 lessons in KLC so far. I noticed that he was very happy with the lessons and now he was much more confident in reading (accurate pronunciation). He is now an English teacher to my daughter and me. Though he had attended only 11 lessons, he had improved a lot in terms of his pronunciation and listening. I hereby would like to thank the teacher’s patient guidance.

Chen Nan Yu

Before Siew Min joined KLC, she was not interested in learning English. She has difficulty pronouncing the words when reading After joining KLC, she is willing to try out the materials that I bought for her which which previously she was reluctant. She can read story books based on her learnings not through memorising. The class has now improved her confidence level in studying and she is also more interested in other subject

Chen Nan Yu

Louis hated English before he joined KLC. Whenever I bought him an English story book he showed no interest at all as he could not read and understand the story. After joining KLC, he changed a lot. He took initiative to read out the story to us. He only needed a little time to revise his spelling now. Wherever he went, he would read out the English words he had seen. He was now totally diffrent from the Old Louis who hated and scared of English. Thanks for KLC English course which had made English easier for kids.
