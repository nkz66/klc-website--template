Franchise Opportunity

Education has been one of the fastest growing industries due to the high demand for it and the private tutor market expected to surpass US$102.8 Billion in 2018. With that in mind, KLC offers a unique product with very little competitor which ensures a high survivability rate.

Our business has a steady growth of more than 60% annually due to the strong demand of quality education.

We Offer

Comprehensive marketing and advertising support

As franchisor, we will be providing assistance, support, and guidance in all aspects of marketing and advertising. Franchisees do not have to carry the burden of marketing and advertising their own branch.

Advance Software Management System

Our software management system allows for an organized day to day operation that includes the handling of :

  1. HR
  2. Scheduling
  3. Inventory
  4. Accounting
  5. Customer Relationship Management
  6. Sales Management

Systematic and structured training methodology

Franchisees will receive complete training and assessments in all aspects of running these business. Therefore inexperience entrepreneurs are more than welcome to step into this amazing industry.

Proprietary syllabus and teaching practices

KLC has developed a unique and perfected formula to teach young children master the foundation of English in an astonishingly short time period. Franchisees will get unprecedented access to this wealth of knowledge to gain a bigger slice of the market cake.

Accomplished business model

Ever since the inception of our business, our business model has not failed to meet our targeted expectations and on top of that the model will be an easy aspect of to execute. In less than a decade, we have bagged prestigious awards which goes to show that this business model is sterling.

Escalating brand presence

We have been featured in various media outlets such as magazines, newspapers, radio channels, social media and online articles. Our company’s brand image and market presence has been growing exponentially without any signs of slowing down.
